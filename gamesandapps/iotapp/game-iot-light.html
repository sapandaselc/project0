About this app

IoT Smart Light is an Android app that lets you control your smart lights remotely over Wi-Fi using IoT protocols. Designed for ESP8266 / ESP32-based smart light setups, the app gives you full control from your phone.

Built as part of an IoT development project, it bridges the gap between hardware and a clean mobile interface — making smart home control simple and accessible.

Key Features
  • 💡Control lights ON/OFF remotely from your phone
  • 🌟Brightness & color control via intuitive sliders
  • ⌛Scheduling — set timers for automatic on/off
  • 📶Real-time status updates over Wi-Fi
  • 🏠Compatible with ESP8266 & ESP32 hardware
  • 🔒Secure local network communication
