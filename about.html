About Your 3D Assets

Free Low Poly 3D Models and Blender Files for Game Developers

Your 3D Assets is a free digital asset library where you can download high-quality low poly 3D models for game development, Blender projects, Unity, and Unreal Engine. Whether you are an indie developer, student, or hobbyist, all assets here are completely free to download and use in your projects.

What we offer:

  • Free Blender (.blend) files — ready to open and edit
  • Free game-ready 3D assets — optimized for real-time engines
  • Free low poly 3D models — lightweight and performance-friendly
  • Trees, weapons, environment assets and more added regularly
  • Compatible with Unity and Unreal Engine

This website is built for game developers, 3D designers, and creators who need ready-to-use free assets without spending hours modeling from scratch. Our goal is to make game development easier and more accessible by providing free, simple, and high-quality 3D game assets anyone can download instantly.
